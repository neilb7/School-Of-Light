Who We Are

We are a India focused, innovative organisation supporting all aspirations towards building great Strategy and Human Resource practices. Through our comprehensive suite of products and services, we build future ready and integrated HR models that are growth focused and inclusive at once.

Simplicity

Simplicity means creating growth that at best is joyful and at least is anxiety free. Cultivating an ability to look at things as they are, without judgment or illusion, and creating a solution that can be commonly understood and implemented.

Integrity

To us, integrity simply means oneness of thought, speech and action. It helps us build clarity, trust and reliability in everything we do.

Inclusive Growth

Long term growth is usually a sum total of thoughts that account for and actions that care for the interests of all stakeholders.

Sustainability is fundamentally an outcome of inclusion. All one needs is a shared belief stronger than the impatience of balance sheets.

Our Services & Products

SoL ACE

SOLACE not only gives you the edge of a ‘cross-industry expert’ and ‘best practice familiar’ HR but also ensures high level of objectivity and transparency. As a part of the scheme, we deploy our consultants on-site, at mutually agreed periodicity to manage, direct & support the Human Resources function.

Code HR

CODE HR is an intuitive HR Management system that has intelligent email integration, flexible reporting & efforless customisation.

SoL AR

Organisations are unique and creating new systems or fine-tuning existing ones in novel environment calls for experience of dealing in them. Strategy, planning, learning & development are some of the areas our Advisory & Research facet covers.

SoL MATE

At SOL MATE, we not only support you to find your capability building needs but also offer Training Analysis, Theatre & Game based training as well as measured training effectiveness.

SoL Search

In a dynamic business scenario, the process of hiring cannot be just about sieving various individuals through a common job description. Its rather a process that seeks to balance and unite the value proposition of all stakeholders. Our long drawn experience in managing internal HR enables us to support you with talent acquisition strategy, search & sourcing.

SoL VE

If you seek innovative training solutions, far reaching communication programs & real time data for meaningful inferences, our wide range of Virtual Engagement offerings can bring you much needed clarity and assurance
